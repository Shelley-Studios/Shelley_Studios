Meet the Characters

The town of Rainbow Valley is home to several animal-like creatures that make up the core cast of our show!

Dean the Bison

Dean is a kind-hearted and gentle bison. He loves to spend time with the other residents. He is especially attached to West the lion, who he cares for like a son.


Joan the Bear

Joan is a wise and compassionate bear. She enjoys drinking tea in the afternoons and reading a good book.
