Meet the Characters

The town of Rainbow Valley is home to several animal-like creatures that make up the core cast of Tales From the Valley. You can learn more about them here!

Dean

Dean is a kind-hearted and gentle bison. He loves to spend time with the other residents. He is especially attached to West, who he cares for like a son. He works at the Rainbow Valley General Store in the mornings.


Joan

Joan is a wise and compassionate bear. She enjoys drinking tea in the afternoons and reading a good book. She loves to work at the library and encourage the other residents to read.


Butch

Butch is a gruff but loyal dog. He likes to walk around the town and tend to the plants in his greenhouse, which he donates to Marvin's resturant.


Penny

Compassionate and sweet, Penny is a deer who loves to bake and tend to flowers in her yard. She works at the Rainbow Valley General Store in the afternoons.


Marvin

While Marvin can be hot-headed, he is also a very meticulous and clever duck. He is a talented chef, and owns the Rainbow Valley Diner with his wife, Angela. When he isn't cooking, he likes to swim in the nearby spring and joke around with his friends.
