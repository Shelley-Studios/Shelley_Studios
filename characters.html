Meet the Characters

The town of Rainbow Valley is home to several animal-like creatures that make up the core cast of Tales From the Valley. You can learn more about them here!

Dean

Dean is a kind-hearted and gentle bison. He loves to spend time with the other residents. He is especially attached to West, who he cares for like a son. Dean also likes to write. He keeps a journal, which he writes in every day. In the mornings, he works at the Rainbow Valley General Store.


Joan

Joan is a wise and intelligent bear. She enjoys drinking tea in the afternoons and reading a good book. She loves to work at the library and encourage the other residents to read.


Butch

Butch is a gruff but loyal dog. He likes to walk around the town and tend to the fruits and vegetables in his greenhouse, which he donates to Marvin's resturant.


Penny

Compassionate and sweet, Penny is a deer who loves to bake and tend to flowers in her yard. She works at the Rainbow Valley General Store in the afternoons.


Marvin

While Marvin can be hot-headed, he is also a very meticulous and clever duck. He is a talented chef, and owns the Rainbow Valley Diner with his wife, Angela. When he isn't cooking, he likes to swim in the nearby spring and joke around with his friends.


Angela

Angela and Marvin are the perfect example of opposites attract. She is a calm and sensible swan, and enjoys working at the Rainbow Valley Diner with her husband. She also likes to paint and make jewelry out of beads and gems.


West

West is the only young resident of Rainbow Valley. He lives with Dean, and loves to explore the valley and take photos with his camera. He is always curious and eager to learn from the other residents.
